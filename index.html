All Things Allstar






Overview

All star cheerleading is one of the fastest growing competetive sports in the world. You can find the sport itself at cheer gyms. What makes all star cheer different is the style of the sport. No cheers all skills. Making your favorite team is hard work. Most of our favorite athletes have moved across the country to be on their dream team. Allstar cheer ranges from levels 1-7. The level you compete on is dependent upon tumbling skills and stunting abilities. Level 1 starts off with basic skills such as cartwheels and ground stunts where as in level 7 you can do basket stunts that allow you to flip in the air. The scope is so broad that there is a level for everyone. To learn even more about allstar cheerleading click on the tabs in the navigation bar. The competetion tab will tell you how competeions operate as they aren't the same as in other sports. It'll also tell you about the most important competitions and how you can compete at them. The notable atheles tab will name some of the most influential and inspirational atheletes in the sport. Many of these athletes have paved and continue to pave the way in the sport. And finally the favorite teams tab will highlight the most popular teams currently dominating the industry.
